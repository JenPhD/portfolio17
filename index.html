About Me

I am a full-stack developer with a PhD in sociology and a previous career in higher education teaching courses such as social psychology, deviance and crime, and medical sociology, among others, and working in a research institute in conjunction with the Florida Department of Health developing hospital mass casualty preparedness and response plans. I have experience with qualitative and quantitative social scientific data coding and analysis. These experiences give me a unique perspective in understanding the needs of customers and clients in various sectors of society, including health care, criminal justice, the workplace, and the marketplace. Besides development, my hobbies and passions include politics and public policy, reading, trivia, and music. An interesting piece of trivia about me: John Pemberton, the pharmacist credited with inventing the original formula for Coca-Cola, is my great great grandfather.
